What We Do

10 Services. One Trusted Team.
Zero Guesswork.

Whether you're building your first home or developing a commercial complex in Chennai, every project needs the right approvals, the right drawings, and the right guidance. Here's exactly what we bring to your table.

01

DTCP / CMDA Building Approval — Chennai

Building in Chennai without a CMDA or DTCP-approved plan isn't just risky — it's illegal. We prepare your complete plan set, handle technical scrutiny, respond to objections, and get your project fully sanctioned. Whether your plot falls under CMDA's jurisdiction (Chennai Metropolitan Area) or DTCP (rest of Tamil Nadu), we know exactly what each office needs and how to get it done the first time.

CMDA · DTCP · Building Plan Sanction · Chennai
02

Corporation Building Permit — Chennai

Building within Greater Chennai Corporation limits? You need a building permit before laying a single brick. We prepare your drawings to meet GCC's technical standards, compile ownership proofs, tax receipts, and supporting documents, and manage the entire submission and follow-up process. We've done this hundreds of times — for homes, shops, apartments, and commercial spaces across Chennai.

GCC · Building Permit · Urban Chennai
03

Municipality Building Approval — Tamil Nadu

Each municipality in Tamil Nadu follows its own building regulations, FSI limits, and setback rules. What works in one town may not work in another. With 6+ years of hands-on experience across municipal offices in the region, we prepare plans that are compliant, submission-ready, and fast-tracked. We handle your entire application — you just sign where needed.

Town Municipalities · Tamil Nadu · Local Body Approval
04

Panchayat Building Approval — Rural Tamil Nadu

Building in a village panchayat or panchayat union area? Approval processes here are different from cities — and often misunderstood. We've helped hundreds of clients across Tamil Nadu secure panchayat construction approvals without costly delays. We know exactly which panchayat office needs which documents, and we manage everything end-to-end so you're never left guessing.

Panchayat Union · Village Construction · Rural Tamil Nadu
05

3D Elevation Design — Chennai

A picture is worth a thousand words — and a 3D elevation can prevent lakhs of rupees in changes later. We create photorealistic exterior elevations that show your home or building exactly as it will look once built. Great for client presentations, investor pitches, or simply helping you make confident design decisions before construction begins. Modern, detailed, and done fast.

3D Elevation · Exterior Rendering · Building Visualization
06

Building Cost Estimation — Chennai

Starting construction without a proper estimate is like going on a road trip without fuel check. Our detailed building estimation covers materials, labour, civil works, finishing, and contingencies — giving you a realistic budget before you spend a single rupee. We follow current Chennai market rates so your estimate is not just accurate, but actually usable for planning and bank loans.

Construction Estimate · Cost Planning · Budget Chennai
07

Structural Drawing & Design — Chennai

A beautiful building is only as good as the structure beneath it. Our certified structural drawings cover RCC column-beam design, footing layouts, slab design, and load calculations — everything your contractor and authority need to build safely. We design for Chennai's specific soil conditions, seismic zone requirements, and IS code standards. Your building's safety starts here.

Structural Drawing · RCC Design · IS Code · Chennai
08

Building Demolition Clearance — Chennai

Demolishing an old structure in Chennai without proper clearance can lead to heavy fines and legal trouble. We handle all regulatory formalities for safe and legal demolition — from authority intimation to structural assessment, safety certifications, and post-demolition documents. Whether it's an old residential house or a commercial building, we ensure the demolition is done by the book.

Building Demolition · Demolition Permit · Chennai
09

Archeological NOC — Tamil Nadu

Constructing near a heritage site, temple, monument, or any protected zone in Tamil Nadu? You'll need a No Objection Certificate from the Archaeological Department before any authority will approve your building plan. This is one of the most misunderstood requirements — and one where delays can stretch for months without the right guidance. We've handled it before, and we'll handle it for you.

Archeological NOC · Heritage Zone Construction · Tamil Nadu
10

Project Brochure & Marketing Material

Selling plots, apartments, or commercial units? A professional brochure builds instant trust with buyers. We design polished real estate brochures that highlight your project's location, features, floor plans, and amenities — combining smart layout design with compelling content that converts. Ideal for builders, developers, and individual plot sellers across Chennai and Tamil Nadu.

How It Works

Simple Process.
Zero Confusion.

We've refined our workflow over 6 years so your experience is smooth from the first call to the final approval stamp.

01
Free Consultation
Call or walk in to our Chrompet office. We understand your plot, project type, and which approval authority applies. Zero charges for the initial discussion.
02
Document Collection
We give you a clear checklist of documents needed — sale deed, patta, EC, tax receipts. No back-and-forth confusion. One clean list, once.
03
Drawing Preparation
Our team prepares building plans, structural drawings, and any additional technical documents as per the authority's requirements and IS code standards.
04
Submission & Follow-up
We submit to the relevant authority — CMDA, Corporation, Municipality or Panchayat — and actively follow up. You stay updated, not anxious.
05
Approval in Hand
We hand you the sanctioned plan with all authority stamps and signatures. Your project is now legally cleared to begin construction. Time to build.
